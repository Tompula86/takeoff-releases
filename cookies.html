Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use on our website, why we use them, and how you can manage their usage.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. Cookies allow the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as language selection, cookie consent options, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter them every time you return to the site.

2. How do we use cookies?

We use cookies for two primary purposes:

  • Necessary Cookies: These are mandatory for the basic operation of the website. We use them to store your cookie consent preferences and to remember your preferred language (Finnish or English). These cookies do not gather information that could be used for marketing or tracking your browsing history.
  • Analytics & Statistical Cookies (Optional): We use these cookies to analyze our website traffic and how users navigate the site. This helps us understand which pages are most popular, where visitors come from, and how we can improve the user experience. All data is collected anonymously.

Cookie Name Type / Service Purpose Lifespan

_ga / _gid Analytics / Google Collects anonymous statistics on how the site is used. Set only if analytics are accepted. 2 years / 24h

4. How can you manage cookies?

You can change your cookie preferences or revoke consent at any time:

  • By clicking on the Cookie Settings link in the footer of the page. This opens the consent banner again, allowing you to edit and save your choices.
  • You can delete all cookies already on your device by clearing your browser's history and cookie cache.
  • You can configure your browser to block cookies entirely, but this may affect website functionality and prevent your preferences (such as language selection) from being saved.
