Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

PQuant ("we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal data when you visit our website or purchase and use our Takeoff desktop application.

1. Data Controller

Company: PQuant Oy
Email: privacy@poitakeoff.com
Address: Helsinki, Finland

2. Categories of Data Collected and Purpose of Processing

We process your personal data in the following situations:

  • License Purchase (Stripe): When you purchase a software license from our site, our payment provider Stripe handles the payment process. We collect your email address to deliver your activation code and invoice.
  • Student License Ordering: If you request a student license, we collect your school email address to verify student status and send the activation code.
  • Contact and Support: If you email us for support, we process your name, email address, and the contents of your message to resolve the support ticket.

3. Local-First Data Principle

The Takeoff desktop application is built as a local-first software. This means:

  • All project data, measurements, drawing files, recipes, and settings are stored locally on your own computer in an SQLite database.
  • No blueprint drawings or takeoff measurements are automatically transferred to cloud storage or to our servers.
  • As the user, you retain complete and exclusive ownership and control over your data files (.ptko).

4. Data Transfers to Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Payment Processing: Stripe Inc. processes payment data securely. We do not store or process your credit card numbers in our systems.
  • Email Services: We use reputable transactional email providers (such as Resend or SendGrid) to dispatch license keys and automated receipts.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information to authorities if required by law.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve our website's functionality and analyze traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences through the "Cookie Settings" link at the bottom of the page. Read more details in our Cookie Policy.

6. Rights of the Data Subject

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access and to receive a copy of the data concerning you.
  • Right to rectify incorrect or incomplete information.
  • Right to demand erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict or object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability.

To exercise your rights, please contact us by email at privacy@poitakeoff.com.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our website and communications are secured using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption.
